About MDC

Sometime in October 2025 4 Min read

My name is Michael David Carty or just call me "MDC", if you see me at an art event.

It would be dishonest to say that I am self taught as my elementary school art teacher Mrs. Klise at Stinson Elementary is the artist who first really showed me what it was to be one. We did simple pieces like how to draw lilies, how to make origami cranes and other typical elementary crafts but it was her level of enthusiasm and the range of art that she showed us that resonated such an energy that in that I saw myself. Also, I loved Ms. Leverton too, my music teacher who would just show us cool music videos.

But back to me. I have doodled just like every child but I became serious about art in high school when teachers got mad at me for drawing on my assignments. Fuck you Mr. Ray. Anyways, my main medium of choice is pen and ink.

In my day work, I am a civil engineer who specializes in the field of Water Resources. I also am a former Marine, where I worked as a weather forecaster in the Reserves for six years before leaving in June 2025.

Earlier this year I started this website not only to showcase my skill but also to break into computer languages and eventually software development. I know that the site could use some polish, but thank you for checking me out!
