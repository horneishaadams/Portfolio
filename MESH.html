Art Review - MESH

Sometime in September, 2025 4 Min read

I recently completed MESH. This is a piece that I have been working on for the past three months. I wanted the focus of the piece to be rhythm. Not necessarily individual repeating elements but the same actions and gestures carried from figure to figure with minor differences. Masculine figures climb over each other, slicing the head off the one above, only to have their own head sliced off by the next. I would not say that my artwork always has an underlying meaning, but a lot of my art reflects my subconscious. I made this piece when I was concerned about my the trajectory of my financial future in such a weird time.
